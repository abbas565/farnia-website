Our Story

Apr 04, 2018- Farnia Jewelry, a family business established in Vancouver in 2018 by Fariba Kakavand, is one of the youngest standing jewellers in British Columbia. Our business has been proudly built on our family values of unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and most importantly, unparalleled customer service. Our mission is to exceed your expectations each and every time while consistently offering exceptional value. We continue to lead the industry by providing unequalled service and selection, along with the most technologically advanced environment in the jewellery industry.
